# MeetingHub-4
## Engineering Requirements Specification (ERS-001)

Version: 1.0
Status: Final — requirements met by hardware v1.0

---

# 1. Objective

Develop an analog equipment capable of connecting four laptops simultaneously using a standard CTIA TRRS headset, allowing:

- audio monitoring from all four sources;
- voice communication on only one laptop at a time;
- quick selection of the microphone source;
- operation without software or drivers.

---

# 2. Functional Requirements

## RF-001 - Inputs

The equipment shall have four 3.5 mm CTIA TRRS inputs for direct connection to laptops.

## RF-002 - Headset

The equipment shall use a single TRRS CTIA headset.

## RF-003 - Monitoring

The user shall simultaneously hear audio from all four laptops.

## RF-004 - Volume control

Each laptop shall have individual volume control.

There shall be an overall volume control.

## RF-005 - Microphone

The user shall select which laptop receives the microphone signal.

## RF-006 - Mute

The equipment shall have a physical MUTE function.

---

# 3. Compatibility

The laptop shall interpret the equipment as a CTIA TRRS headset connected directly.

The following shall not be required:

- driver installation;
- additional software;
- special configuration.

---

# 4. Durability Requirements

The equipment shall be designed for heavy use.

Target:

- > 1 million operations of the microphone selector;
- metal panel connectors;
- industrial components.

---

# 5. Constraints

Do not use:

- Bluetooth;
- USB audio;
- firmware;
- digital processing;
- applications.

---

# 6. Power Supply

Input:

USB-C 5V.

Use:

- active mixer;
- headphone amplifier.

---

# 7. Acceptance Criteria

The prototype will be approved when:

- four laptops work simultaneously;
- the headset is recognized normally;
- the microphone works on the selected laptop;
- no intervention in Windows is required.
